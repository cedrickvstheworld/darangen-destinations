LANAO PEOPLE'S PARK AND PUBLIC LIBRARY

Lanao People's Park, Barrio Saber, Marawi City, Lanao del Sur

Lanao People's Park and Public Library is a place where people of all ages could and walks of life could gather and share their cultures with each other by utilizing its facilities and events such as books, bazars, and the park intself. On June 16, 1992 the administrative jurisdiction of the park was transfered from the Lanao Park & Library Foundation and the Maranao Heritage Foundation to the Provincial Government of Lanao del Sur.


Mode of Transport:

Bus ( 1 hr. 45 min. ) / Jeepney ( 1 hr. 55 min. ) / Tricycle ( 2hrs. 20 min. )
